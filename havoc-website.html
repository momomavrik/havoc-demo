HavocOS — The Autonomy Stack

Software-Defined Hardware. Every Platform.

Real-time decision-making and control for full collaborative autonomy. The Havoc stack fuses sensing, planning, and execution — enabling heterogeneous assets to self-organize and complete complex missions with minimal human supervision. Modular. Platform-agnostic. Field-proven.

  • Runs unmodified across 100+ air, surface, and ground platforms
  • 25,000+ hours of autonomous testing across real-world contested environments

Havoc Control — Fleet Command

One Operator. Thousands of Assets.

An intuitive, mission-focused interface that lets a single operator command, monitor, and re-task entire autonomous fleets in real time. Designed to collapse the distance between human intent and machine execution — without sacrificing situational awareness.

  • Single UI window for fleet-scale supervision and real-time re-tasking
  • Human-on-the-loop — amplifies oversight, never replaces the operator
